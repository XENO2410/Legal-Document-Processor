Petitioner: Advocate Arjun Mehta
Respondent: Union of India

Filed under Article 32 of the Constitution of India.

Advocate Arjun Mehta, a practicing lawyer in Delhi, files this Public Interest Litigation (PIL) in the Supreme Court of India, seeking the protection of the environment and challenging the Union of India's failure to enforce the provisions of the Environment (Protection) Act, 1986, in relation to air pollution in Delhi.
